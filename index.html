Life Journey

Birth & Early Life

Late Michael Ngige Mwaura was born in 1943 to the late Mwaura Njuguna and the late Hannah Wanjiku at Mang'u Kawira Village. He was raised in accordance with Kikuyu traditions and grew up with his siblings: Teresia Gathoni, the late Mary Wanjiru, and the late Njuguna. He was brought up with strong morals and values.

Education

He began his education in 1951 and studied up to Form 6 (A-Level). His academic journey laid the foundation for his future success and wisdom.

Work & Career

He was employed in 1969, working at UTI Company in Thika until 1973. He later joined Kenya Canners, where he worked until 1990. After formal employment, he engaged in business, demonstrating his entrepreneurial spirit.

Marriage & Family

He had three wives:

  • Cecilia Mukuhi
  • Mary Wakonyo
  • Ruth Wambui

He was blessed with 13 children: The late Gabriel Mwaura, the late Rufas Mwaura, the late Hannah Wanjiku, Moses Njuguna, Mary Wanjiru, Naomi Wanjiku, Rachel Waithera, Beatrice Njoki, Jacinta Mwihaki, Beth Wanjiru, Boniface Ngotho, Rhoda Wanjiru, and Jackson Waweru.

Faith & Church

He was a devoted member of Kiwanuka Catholic Church, Thika Kiganjo, where he served faithfully and participated actively in church activities.

Final Journey

He fell ill in mid-2023 and received treatment at several hospitals, including Thika Level 5 Hospital. On 5th January 2026, his condition worsened, and he was admitted again at Thika Level 5 Hospital, where he peacefully passed away, surrounded by loved ones.
